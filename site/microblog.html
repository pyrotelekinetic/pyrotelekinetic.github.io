static urls

2023-04-20, 12:45

One can easily view older commits of pyrosite. However, there is currently no way to view any commit but the latest in rendered form.

What if I had nix build pyrosite at every commit, and place them in subdirectories named by the commit hash? I think this could work.

The primary benefit of this is the resulting static urls. Pyrosite pages could be linked to at any specified commit, and they would be rendered displayed correctly.
